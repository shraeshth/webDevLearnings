Quick Hummus Recipe

Ingredients

  • 400g chickpeas
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Tahini

Instructions

  1. Peel and chop garlic
  2. Add ingredients to processor
  3. Blend to desired consistency
    • Coarse: short blend
    • Smooth: longer blend

Storage

Store in fridge for 7 days or freeze up to 2 months.
